# Grot-lash

---

# Abilities

The bearer gains the following ability: 
>**Grot Lash (Aura):** While a friendly GRETCHIN INFANTRY unit is within 3" of any models equipped with a grot-lash, [[add 1" to the Move characteristic::highlight]] of models in that unit.
