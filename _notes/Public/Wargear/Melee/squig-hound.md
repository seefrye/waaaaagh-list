# Squig Hound

---

# Abilities

The bearer gains the following ability: 
>**Squig Hound \[Aura\]:** While a friendly GRETCHIN INFANTRY unit is within 3" of the bearer, models in that unit ignore modifiers to Combat Attrition tests.
